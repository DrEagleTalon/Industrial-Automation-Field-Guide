## Description

Explains differences, advantages, and proper selection criteria between fuses and circuit breakers for industrial applications.

## Core Concepts

### What is it?

Devices used for overcurrent protection, safeguarding electrical systems from damage due to short circuits and overloads.

### How it works

- **Fuses:** Melt internal elements to interrupt current.
    
- **Breakers:** Mechanically interrupt current, resettable.
    

### When to Use

Fuses are suitable for sensitive or critical circuits, breakers are ideal for convenience and maintenance ease.
